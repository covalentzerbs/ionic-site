privacy.

ionic post keeps this simple.

this website collects almost nothing. there are no accounts, no forms, no email capture, and no payments here. we never sell your data, we never share it with advertisers, and we never will.

here is the complete picture:

google analytics. we use it to see which pages get visited, roughly where visitors come from, and what kind of device they use. it is aggregate and anonymous to us. we look at it to make the site better, that is the whole story.

vimeo. our films are hosted on vimeo and load with do-not-track enabled, which tells vimeo not to collect viewing analytics on you.

fonts. type is served via google fonts, which involves google's servers receiving your ip address to deliver the files.

that is everything. if you have a question about any of it, email hello@ionicpost.co and a human will answer.
